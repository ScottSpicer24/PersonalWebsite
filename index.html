My Story

In the fall of 2017 I decided to persue a degree in industrial engineering at the University of Central Florida and started learning about manufacturing, statistics and process improvement. While pursuing that degree I interned at the Frito-Lay manufacturing plant in Casa Grande, Arizona and worked to reduce their water usage. After I graduated in December of 2021 I started working at the Frito-Lay manufacturing plant in Orlando on the night shift managing their maintenance team.

After I had started working there for a while I quickly realized that this was more of a management position and less of and engineering one so I started thinking about getting back into engineering. While contemplating this I remembered how my favorite classes in college were my CS classes. As a result I started to teach myself programming while working. I enjoyed it so much that I ultimately decided to quit my job and go back to school for computer science to persue it full time.
